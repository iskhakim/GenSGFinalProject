About Us

Paws & Whiskers was founded by the Tan family as a small, family-run initiative. Over the years, it has grown into a beloved pet store chain that caters to the needs of pet owners across the community. Our mission is to provide premium-quality pet food, accessories, and care products to ensure the happiness and health of your furry companions. We take pride in offering a curated selection of products that combine quality, sustainability, and style.

At Paws & Whiskers, we are passionate about pets and committed to creating a community of pet lovers. Our team works tirelessly to source the best products and provide exceptional customer service. Whether you are looking for everyday essentials or specialty items, we are here to help you and your pets every step of the way.
